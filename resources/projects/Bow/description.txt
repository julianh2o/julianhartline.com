Click and drag from within the green zones to launch arrows.
Headshots award bonus points. Use cash to buy upgrades and
ammo from the store. Multi-shots and volleys can be used for greater firepower at the cost of arrows!<br>

space - multishot
z - volley
p - pause
/ - store
